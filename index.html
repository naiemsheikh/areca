Discover Areca Leaf Plates Story

The use of areca leaf plates can be traced back to ancient civilizations in India and Southeast Asia, where the areca palm tree is native. The leaves of the areca palm tree have been traditionally used for various purposes, including as plates and bowls for food. The leaves are strong, durable and naturally water-resistant, making them an ideal material for plates. The process of making areca leaf plates involves cleaning, boiling, and shaping the leaves into plates, which are then dried and polished. The use of areca leaf plates has been passed down through generations and continues to be a popular choice for eco-friendly and sustainable tableware.

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide eco-friendly products that make a positive impact on the environment. One of our most popular products is our line of areca plates. These plates are made from the leaves of the areca palm tree, a renewable resource that is biodegradable and compostable. This means that unlike traditional disposable plates made of plastic or paper, our areca plates won't contribute to pollution or harm wildlife.

Our Service

Our areca plates are not only good for the earth, but they are also functional and stylish. They are perfect for restaurants, catering companies, and households looking for a sustainable alternative for their events and gatherings. We are committed to making a difference, and it is our mission to provide high-quality eco-friendly products that are accessible to everyone. By choosing our areca plates, you can reduce your environmental footprint and make a positive impact on the planet.
